Slow Cooker Ribs

Zap Zoop! These freaki deakies will light up your life

Ingredients

  • Ribs for about 6 regular people or 3 major mondos
  • Seasonings: Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Barbecue Sauce: Whatever kind you like

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Add all the ingredients (besides the sauce) to the slow cooker.
  2. Step 2: Cook on Low for 8 hours or High for 4 hours.
  3. Step 3: Coat the ribs with sauce and bake until the sauce caramelizes.

Thats it!
