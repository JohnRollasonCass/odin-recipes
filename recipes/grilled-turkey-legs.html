Grilled Turkey Legs

o0o0o these suckers have that wham zam that'll leave you thinking "woowee"

Sweet, spicy, you'll be reliving every great state fair moment the second

slam one of these babies into your face

ingredients:

  • 1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 4 Turkey legs
  • 2 table spoons of honey

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat grill to high heat and oil lightly
  2. Step 2: In a large pot, mix the lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage, sugar, hot sauce, red pepper, pepper, and onion. Place the turkey legs in the mixture, and bring to a boil. Cook 30 to 45 minutes, until the turkey has reached an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (80 degrees C).
  3. Step 3: Remove onion slices from the mixture, and arrange on the prepared grill. Place turkey legs over the onions. Drizzle with honey, and season with steak seasoning. Cook, tuning once, 20 minutes, or until a crisp browned crust has formed on the turkey legs.

Thats it!
